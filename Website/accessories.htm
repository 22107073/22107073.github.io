What is a Mobile accessory?

A mobile accessory is almost like a cosmetic, like a case, stickers maybe even a pop socket, mobile accessories are little things you can add to your phone to express your self and make it to your own look.

You can design your own by doing it by hand or you could pop into a local phone store and find some there, local stores like a pound store or a mobile store always tend to have mobile accessorys
